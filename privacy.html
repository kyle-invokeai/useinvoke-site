Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our communication automation services. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Business name, email address, and phone numbers used for service configuration.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, API calls, and system performance metrics.
  • Communication Metadata: Message timestamps, delivery status, and routing information required for service operation.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and cookies used for security and service improvement.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our communication automation services
  • Process and route messages and calls through our infrastructure
  • Monitor service performance and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Ensure compliance with telecommunications regulations and legal requirements
  • Communicate with you about service updates, security alerts, and support matters
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to our systems

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our platform (e.g., telecommunications carriers, cloud infrastructure providers, security services).
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate confidentiality protections.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Communications Compliance

Invoke operates in strict compliance with telecommunications and messaging regulations. Users must obtain proper consent before using our platform to send SMS communications. Invoke does not support or enable unsolicited messaging, spam, or robocalling. All communications sent through our platform must comply with applicable laws including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), CAN-SPAM Act, and other relevant regulations.
